PLACE

I was born and raised in the city of Ponorogo, a city famous for the art of Reog Ponorogo. The name Ponorogo itself according to history comes from the word "Pramana" which means power, secret of life, and "Raga" which means body, body. These two words can be interpreted that behind the human body is hidden a secret of life in the form of steady mental cultivation related to self-control. 4 interesting things in Ponorogo are Reog Ponorogo, Grebeg Suro memorial, City of Santri, and Ponorogo chicken satay, as well as Ngebel Lake.
